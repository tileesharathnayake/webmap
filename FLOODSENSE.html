Advanced Flood Monitoring & Analysis System

A comprehensive geospatial solution for flood risk assessment and monitoring in the Kurunegala district. Access detailed maps and analysis tools to understand flood patterns, terrain characteristics, and infrastructure vulnerability.

Interactive Mapping Dashboard

🌊

Flood Risk Assessment

Real-time flood risk zones with historical data from 2015-2024. Covers 1,693 km² of Kurunegala district with early warning systems for 47 Grama Niladhari divisions.

👥

Population Distribution

Detailed demographic mapping of 1.68 million residents across urban and rural areas. Identifies 125,000 people in high-risk flood zones requiring priority evacuation planning.

🌧️

Rainfall Pattern Analysis

Historical rainfall data showing average annual precipitation of 1,200-2,500mm. Tracks monsoon patterns and extreme weather events with 15 automated weather stations.

🛣️

Road Network Infrastructure

Comprehensive mapping of 2,400km road network including A-grade, B-grade, and rural roads. Identifies 89 flood-prone bridges and alternative evacuation routes.

⛰️

Slope & Terrain Analysis

Digital terrain modeling showing elevation ranges from 30m to 1,200m above sea level. Critical slope analysis for landslide risk assessment in 23 vulnerable areas.

🗻

Digital Elevation Model

High-resolution DEM with 10m accuracy covering the entire district. Essential for watershed analysis, flood modeling, and infrastructure planning with contour intervals.

💧

Water Bodies & Drainage

Mapping of 127 water bodies including Deduru Oya, Mee Oya rivers, 45 tanks, and extensive canal network. Real-time water level monitoring at 12 key locations.

24/7 monitoring network with automated alerts sent to 15 emergency response centers. SMS warnings reach 95% of at-risk population within 30 minutes of flood detection.
